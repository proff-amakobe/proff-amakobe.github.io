About Me

Dr. Amakobe is an accomplished academic and industry professional with extensive experience in computer science, software development, and data analytics. Currently serving as a Graduate Adjunct Professor at Trine University and an Adjunct Professor at the University of the Cumberlands, Dr. Amakobe actively engages students with a genuine enthusiasm for their learning, providing thorough feedback and fostering independent research projects.

With a robust teaching portfolio, Dr. Amakobe has designed and implemented innovative curricula across multiple institutions, including George Mason University and Southern New Hampshire University. Dr. Amakobe has developed and taught courses in Blockchain, Data Analytics, Big Data, and Database Management, utilizing cutting-edge technologies such as PyTorch, TensorFlow, and SQL.

In parallel with an academic career, Dr. Amakobe has held significant roles in the industry, demonstrating a strong track record in leadership and project management. As a Principal Engineer and Software Development Manager at T-Mobile, Dr. Amakobe led key DevOps initiatives, optimized supply chain processes using Blockchain, and managed teams to deliver high-quality software solutions. Previous positions at Accenture, Mindtree, Wipro Ltd., West Monroe Partners, and Deloitte Consulting LLP highlight Dr. Amakobe's expertise in solution architecture, DevOps, and technical leadership.

Dr. Amakobe holds a Doctor of Computer Science and a Master of Science in Computer Science from Colorado Technical University, where he conducted qualitative studies to enhance healthcare data management and explore the impact of big data analytics in various industries. Dr. Amakobe is pursuing a Doctor of Education in Educational Leadership at Maryville University, focusing on integrating deep learning methodologies into computer science pedagogy to better prepare students for the evolving job market.

A prolific author and speaker, Dr. Amakobe has published notable works on applying blockchain technology in healthcare and the impact of big data in banking. He has presented at various tech conferences, including a Blockchain Tech-Talk at KIPNA in 2022.

With certifications in Agile Project Management, Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, and Blockchain technologies, Dr. Amakobe continues contributing to academic and professional communities, inspiring the next generation of computer science professionals through innovative teaching and practical industry insights.
